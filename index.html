Call of Victory

Step into the battlefield, lead your troops to victory, and experience the ultimate military strategy game!

About the game

  • Call of Victory immerses players in a world of high-stakes military strategy where every decision can change the outcome of a battle.

    As the commander of an elite squad, you will engage in real-time combat across dynamic and diverse environments, ranging from urban warzones to remote outposts.

    The game is designed to challenge your tactical thinking, offering a variety of combat situations where you’ll need to use your resources wisely, coordinate your units effectively, and outsmart opponents.

  • Creating Call of Victory was no easy task for the development team.

    One of the biggest challenges was perfecting the balance between strategic depth and accessibility.

    They wanted the game to appeal to both casual players and strategy enthusiasts, so they focused on creating intuitive controls without sacrificing the complexity of real-time combat.

  • The game is not only about surviving on the battlefield—it’s about leading your team to ultimate victory.

    To do so, you must master the art of resource management, unit placement, and battle timing.

    Each mission is a test of your military prowess and leadership skills, and every choice you make will have lasting consequences.

    Whether you’re playing the single-player campaign or diving into multiplayer PvP modes, Call of Victory offers a complex and satisfying strategy experience that keeps players on their toes.

How to play

    • Assemble Your Troops:

      Recruit and upgrade a wide variety of military units, including infantry, tanks, and air support.

    • Complete Missions:

      Embark on exciting missions, each with unique objectives and challenges to test your combat skills.

    • Upgrade and Evolve:

      Enhance your units and weapons with powerful upgrades to keep up with increasingly difficult enemies.

    • Strategic Battles:

      Command your forces in real-time tactical battles, utilizing terrain, positioning, and timing to outsmart opponents.

    • PvP Mode:

      Challenge other players in intense PvP (Player vs Player) battles, or team up with friends to dominate the leaderboard.
